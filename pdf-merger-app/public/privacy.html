Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 28, 2025

PDF Merger Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

Information We Collect

When you use our PDF merging tool, all processing happens locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your PDF files or their contents.

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our service, such as which features are used most frequently. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

Personal Data

We only collect personal data if you voluntarily provide it, such as when you contact us via email. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiries and is not shared with third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on our website. Your privacy is our priority, and we've designed our tool to function without any tracking mechanisms.

Data Storage and Security

Your files are processed locally in your browser and are never stored on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to our systems.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or error tracking that would collect information about your usage of our tool.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@pdfmergerpro.com
